[Hook]
I'm caught in the pull of your eyes tonight
We're bathed in the glow of the porch string lights
Your fingers are drawing a map on my skin
And I'm ready to lose just to let you win

[Verse 1]
The shadows are dancing across the room
I'm breathing the sweet of your midnight perfume
You lean in so close I can feel the heat
And every slow step brings you closer to me

[Pre-Chorus]
The whole world is quiet, just look at us now
I want to fall in, but I don't know how
To tell you the way that my heartbeat runs
When you look at me like I'm the only one

[Chorus]

You're pulling me close with a single glance
And I'm falling right into this slow-burning dance
